Operationalization & Testing

From Contract to Live System in 2–4 Weeks

Phase 1: Contracting & Privacy

Clients contract directly with their identity provider (e.g. TransUnion) and separately with DeliverAI. Identity resolution occurs upstream. DeliverAI never receives PII , only anonymous, resolved identifiers.

Privacy by Design

No names, emails, IP addresses, MAIDs, or raw device data ever enter DeliverAI systems. This structure materially simplifies InfoSec, procurement, and regulatory review.

Phase 2: Environment Stand-Up

DeliverAI provisions a dedicated, tenant-isolated environment. Clients securely log into DeliverAI to view their data. No client infrastructure deployment required.

Phase 3: Content Identification

A new tagging structure assigns persistent Content IDs to all creatives, enabling cross-channel tracking of messages, formats, and creators.

Phase 4: Creative Intelligence

All creative assets are deconstructed using 20+ research-backed signals. Performance benchmarks are established immediately without historical data dependency.

Phase 5: Attribution & Testing

Machine learning attributes outcomes to content exposure. Continuous observational testing replaces slow, disruptive A/B experimentation.

Live attribution, insights, and optimization recommendations are typically available within 2–4 weeks.
