Founder–Problem Fit

15 Years of Solving “Impossible” Systems

This product hasn’t existed before because the problem doesn’t live in one discipline. Marketing measurement evolved in silos — brand lived in retrospective MMM, performance in last-click attribution, creative in qualitative judgment, and identity inside walled platforms. No system was designed to connect creative → exposure → identity → perception → outcome at the individual level.

The Content Mix Model is not a single breakthrough idea — it is the synthesis of systems that historically had no reason to converge.
