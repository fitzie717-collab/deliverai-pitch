The Problem

Media measurement is stuck in a platform-first world.

1. No online to offline resolution

Dark channels like Radio, Audio, and OOH have no direct link to web sales, leaving massive spend unoptimized and unproven.

2. No creative analysis at scale

The "Black Box" of walled gardens hides creative DNA. Brands don't know why an ad worked, only that it did (or didn't).

3. Expensive to engage and measure across channels

Fragmented attribution tools and high bot-traffic vulnerability make omnichannel scaling inefficient and prohibitively costly.

4. No one stop shop solution

Brand health metrics (lift, sentiment) are siloed from hard sales data, preventing a unified view of long-term and short-term ROI.
